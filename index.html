A Warm Irish Welcome

Nestled in the heart of the city, O'Donnell's brings the heart and soul of Ireland to your doorstep. Enjoy hearty food, live music, and perfectly poured pints — just like back home. Whether you're here for the match or a meal with friends, you'll always find a warm welcome waiting.
